Before continuing, please familiarize yourself with our rules:

R1. Don't abuse exploits.

If you find a flaw in the service, please contact us at staff@computernewb.com. Abusing exploits will result in a permanent ban.

R2. Don't impersonate other users.

Impersonating other members is not allowed. You will be banned.

R3. One vote per person.

You may not abuse any methods which let you vote more than once. Violators will be banned.

R4. No Remote Administration Tools.

Don't abuse tools like TeamViewer to unfairly take control over the VM. Wait your turn like everyone else.

R5. Don't bypass CollabNet.

Sites are blocked for a reason. If you think a website shouldn't be blocked, send us an email at staff@computernewb.com.

R6. No toxicity.

Toxicity is not allowed. This would include actions like purposefully destroying a user's session, attacking them based on their characteristics ("hate speech"), or just generally being a jerk.

NSFW Warning

While we give a good effort to keep NSFW content off the main VMs through content blocking and moderation, people will occasionally slip it through. Please use your best judgement and do not use CollabVM in an environment where such material should not be seen.

In addition, using CollabVM to violate US federal law or NJ state law will result in an immediate permanent ban, and your actions reported if necessary.
